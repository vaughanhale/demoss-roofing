About

Are you looking for a roofing contractor in Wellington?

If you are looking for a roofer anywhere the in the Greater Wellington Region, DeMoss Roofing is your best option. Not only are you presented with a one stop shop for all roofing services, from roofing repairs and roof restoration to guttering services and new roof installations, but a service that encompasses Upper Hutt, Lower Hutt, Wellington, Kapiti and everywhere in Greater Wellington.

With over 40 years joint experience and specialising in roof restorations, you can expect the team to take your roof restoration and renovations services seriously and provide a professional service. From waterproofing to roof cleaning and roof painting, rest assured that DeMoss Roofing provide the service you are looking for.

If you ever have a problem with your roof, or worry that it is starting to leak and create problems in your home, the DeMoss team can also provide the solution. By coming in and assessing the situation, they can communicate the problem to you and provide a roofing repairs service that effectively rids the problem.

You can expect the team to help you with all new roofing installations for new homes and renovations as well. Its important that you use professional roofers when installing a new roof so you can be sure there are no problems. DeMoss Roofing will help you through the whole process and – also provide great after sales care if you even encounter any problems.

With guttering and downpipe repair and replacement services, you really get all your roofing services from DeMoss Roofing. Contact us today for a free quote or to talk to an expert about your requirements – we are always contactable and look forward to your call!
